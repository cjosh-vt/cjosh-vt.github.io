Links to projects that I care about

Below are links to a few projects that I use frequently and care about deeply, feel free to take a look.

Folding@Home

Folding@home is a project focused on disease research. The problems they’re solving require many computer calcul­ations – and they need your help to find the cures!The Folding@home software allows you to share your unused computer power with researchers to find potential cures.

Learn more

Arch Linux

Arch is a distribution of Linux that prouds itself on being very lightweight and simple. This, however, does make it rathe beginner unfriendly. If you are new to Linux this should not be your first distribution, but if you have dipped your feet into the world of linux and want to know how it works, installing Arch Linux will be sure to teach you something.

Learn more

Jellyfin

Jellyfin is the volunteer-built media solution that puts you in control of your media. Stream to any device from your own server, with no strings attached. Your media, your server, your way.

Learn more

Nextcloud

Nextcloud is a suite of client-server software for creating and using file hosting services. Nextcloud is free and open-source, which means that anyone is allowed to install and operate it on their own private server devices.
